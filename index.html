Data Privacy

What is Data Privacy and why it’s so important Data Privacy also refereed to as information privacy or data protection. It is the ability to interact with data, the internet, and other technologies while maintaining the security and privacy of that information when interacting with it. This means understanding what can be shared or disseminated and what cannot. Data Privacy governs how data is collected, shared and used however, data security protects data from compromise by external attackers and malicious insiders.
You, as an individual, have a lot at stake when it comes to Data Privacy. The more you know about it, the better able you’ll be to help protect yourself from a large number of risks.
When data that should be kept private gets in the wrong hands, bad thing can happen so therefore data is one of the most important assets a companies has. With the rise of the data economy, companies find enormous value in collecting, sharing and using data. Also, privacy is the right of and individual to be free from uninvited surveillance. To safely exist in one’s space and freely express one’s opinion behind closed door is critical to living in a democratic society.
Some of the tips to protect your privacy are: before discarding, shared documents including receipts and bank and credit card statements, that contain personal information and use strong, unique passwords for all your online accounts. So this is all about Data privacy and why Data Privacy is important for everyone.
